About Me

Hey, I'm Dev Raj Malik, also known as Roar The Gamedev — a self-made, multi-disciplinary creator from India. I'm 19 years old with over 7 years of experience crafting digital experiences that blend technology, creativity, and emotion.

I'm the Founder & Director of Roar Studios, and I specialize in Android app development and web development — freelancing part-time to bring bold ideas to life. I'm currently a student pursuing a B.A. (Hons) in History, balancing my academic journey with a passion for creation.

My skillset spans HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C# and Python, and my toolbox extends into UI/UX design, game development, music composition, and commercial writing.

I started young, building indie video games with nothing but passion and code. Since then, I've grown into a full-stack thinker — designing sleek, functional apps, building expressive websites, and telling stories through interfaces, graphics, and words.

My creations stand out for their originality, clean design, and seamless user experience — not because I follow trends, but because I build with purpose. Every project is a chance to innovate, connect, and make something that matters.

Whether it's an app, a brand identity, a browser-based experience, or an idea that doesn't fit a mold — I'm here to make it real.
